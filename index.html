My BirthPlace
Baguio City, located in the Cordillera mountains, is known as the "Summer Capital of the Philippines" due to its cool climate, lush landscapes, and rich cultural tapestry. Its attractions include Burnham Park, Session Road, vibrant markets, Mines View Park, Botanical Garden, and Mount Pulag's vistas. Baguio City offers a blend of beauty, culture, and warmth, making it a popular destination for travelers.

My University
The University of the Cordilleras (UC) is a leading institution in the Philippines, known for its academic excellence and cultural heritage. With over seven decades of experience, UC offers a diverse range of programs in arts, sciences, business, law, engineering, and technology. Its state-of-the-art facilities and resources foster a vibrant campus community, empowering students to become socially responsible leaders.
